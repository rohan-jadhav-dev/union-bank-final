Union Bank of India — Branch Staff Reference Guide

ACCOUNT TYPES AND FEATURES

Savings Account (Regular):
Minimum balance: ₹1,000 (urban), ₹500 (semi-urban), ₹0 (rural/PMJDY)
Interest rate: 2.75% per annum
Features: Debit card, internet banking, mobile banking, cheque book
Documents required: Aadhaar card, PAN card, passport photo, address proof

Current Account:
Minimum balance: ₹10,000 (urban), ₹5,000 (semi-urban)
No interest paid on balance
Features: Unlimited transactions, overdraft facility, trade finance
For: Business owners, traders, companies

Fixed Deposit (FD) Rates (2026):
7 days to 45 days: 3.00%
46 days to 90 days: 4.50%
91 days to 180 days: 5.50%
181 days to 1 year: 6.00%
1 year to 2 years: 7.00%
2 years to 3 years: 6.75%
Senior citizen additional: 0.50% extra

Recurring Deposit (RD):
Minimum: ₹100 per month
Tenure: 6 months to 10 years
Rate: Same as FD for equivalent tenure

LOAN PRODUCTS

Home Loan:
Interest rate: 8.50% to 9.75% p.a. (floating, based on CIBIL)
Maximum amount: ₹2 crore (urban), ₹75 lakh (semi-urban)
Tenure: Up to 30 years
Processing fee: 0.50% of loan amount (max ₹15,000)
Documents: Income proof (3 months salary slip or 2 years ITR), property documents, Aadhaar, PAN, bank statement (6 months)
CIBIL score required: Minimum 700

Personal Loan:
Interest rate: 10.50% to 14.00% p.a.
Maximum amount: ₹15 lakh
Tenure: 1 to 5 years
Processing fee: 1% of loan amount
Documents: Salary slip (3 months), bank statement (6 months), Aadhaar, PAN
CIBIL score required: Minimum 650

Education Loan:
For studies in India: Up to ₹10 lakh
For studies abroad: Up to ₹20 lakh
No collateral for loans up to ₹7.5 lakh
Interest rate: 9.00% to 11.00% p.a.
Moratorium: Course period + 1 year

Gold Loan:
Per gram: ₹3,500 to ₹4,800 (based on purity)
LTV: Up to 75% of gold value
Tenure: Up to 12 months
Interest rate: 8.80% p.a.
Processing fee: ₹250 flat

DIGITAL BANKING SERVICES

Union Bank Mobile App (Union Mobile):
Features: Account balance, mini statement, fund transfer, bill payment, FD booking, loan EMI payment
Registration: Mobile number linked to account required

Internet Banking:
Registration: Visit branch with account number + registered mobile
Features: NEFT, RTGS, IMPS transfers, statement download, cheque stop, FD management

UPI:
VPA format: mobilenumber@unionbank
Limit: ₹1 lakh per transaction, ₹5 lakh per day

TRANSFER LIMITS AND TIMINGS

NEFT:
Timings: Monday–Saturday 8 AM to 7 PM (except 2nd and 4th Saturday, holidays)
Batches every 30 minutes
Limit: No upper limit (bank may set internal limits)

RTGS:
Timings: Monday–Friday 7 AM to 6 PM, Saturday 7 AM to 1 PM
Minimum amount: ₹2 lakh
Suitable for high-value transfers

IMPS:
Available 24×7 including holidays
Limit: ₹5 lakh per transaction
Charges: ₹2.50 to ₹15 based on amount

KYC REQUIREMENTS

Full KYC (for regular accounts):
- Proof of Identity: Aadhaar / Passport / Voter ID / Driving License
- Proof of Address: Aadhaar / Utility bill (not older than 2 months) / Bank statement
- Recent photograph (passport size)
- PAN card (mandatory for transactions above ₹50,000)

Re-KYC:
Required every 2 years for high-risk customers
Every 8 years for low-risk customers
Can be done at branch or via Video KYC

Video KYC:
Available on Union Bank app
Documents: Original Aadhaar must be shown on camera
No branch visit required
